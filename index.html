Fractured Reality is an exploration game like no other

About

Fractured Reality is the working title for a game created by Alex Jordan and Max Heim-Salgado. In Fractured Reality, you are placed into a mysterious, randomly-generated world with no solid ground. You must explore the world, finding fantastic lands and characters along the way. If you want, you can even choose your destiny.

Or not. It's your choice.

Authors

Fractured Reality is being produced by Alex Jordan, who came up with the concept and did the programming, with extensive artistic and conceptual help from Max Heim-Salgado.
